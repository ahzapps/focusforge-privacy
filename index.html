Privacy Policy

Focus Forge  ·  Last updated: May 17, 2026

Focus Forge ("the App") is operated by Asad Zafar. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. By using the App you agree to the practices described here.

1. Age Requirement

Focus Forge is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

2. Information We Collect

2a. Information You Provide via Google Sign-In

When you choose to sign in with Google to access the leaderboard, Google shares the following with us:

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture URL

Sign-in is optional. Core app features work without an account.

2b. Leaderboard Data

If you opt in to the leaderboard, we store the following in Google Firestore:

  • Your display name and profile picture (from Google)
  • Your in-game level and experience points (XP)
  • Aggregate session statistics (e.g. total focus time)

This data is visible to other players on the leaderboard.

2c. Purchase Information

In-app purchases are processed through the Google Play Store. We use RevenueCat to verify and manage subscription/purchase status. RevenueCat receives a purchase receipt from Google Play — we do not store your payment card details. RevenueCat's privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.

2d. Accessibility Service

The App's App Blocking feature uses Android's Accessibility Service to detect which app is in the foreground, so it can enforce your session whitelist and Hardcore Mode blocked-apps list. Specifically:

  • The service reads foreground app package names only — it does not read screen content, text fields, passwords, or any other on-screen data.
  • This data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
The Accessibility Service is used solely to enforce app-blocking rules you configure. It is not used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond this feature.

2e. Local Data

All game progress (dungeon progress, inventory, HP, coins, settings) is stored locally on your device using Android's storage. This data never leaves your device unless you opt in to leaderboard sync.

2f. Notifications

The App sends local notifications (e.g. focus reminders). These are generated on-device and no notification content is sent to our servers.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your profile and ranking on the leaderboard (Google account data)
  • To verify and unlock premium features (RevenueCat purchase data)
  • To enforce your app-blocking preferences (Accessibility Service)
  • To operate and improve the App

We do not sell your data. We do not use your data for advertising.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase / Firestore — leaderboard database. Privacy policy
  • Google Sign-In — optional authentication. Privacy policy
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management. Privacy policy
  • Google Play — app distribution and payment processing. Privacy policy

5. Data Retention

Leaderboard data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us (see below) or by signing out, which removes your data from the leaderboard.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent (e.g. sign out to stop leaderboard sync)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures (Firebase security rules, HTTPS) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable precautions to safeguard your information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
