Data Deletion Request

Focus Forge  ·  Operated by Asad Zafar

You can request the deletion of your account and all associated data from Focus Forge at any time.

What will be deleted

  • Your Google account profile (display name, email, profile picture) stored in our database
  • Your leaderboard entry and associated game statistics

What is not affected

  • Local game data stored on your device — this can be cleared by uninstalling the app
  • Purchase records held by Google Play and RevenueCat — contact those services directly to manage that data

How to request deletion

Send an email to the address below with the subject line "Data Deletion Request" and include the Google account email address associated with your Focus Forge account. We will process your request within 30 days.

You can also sign out of your Google account within the app (Settings screen) to immediately remove your data from the leaderboard without contacting us.
Email us to request deletion
